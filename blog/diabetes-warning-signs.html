Early Warning Signs of Diabetes You Shouldn't Ignore

Common early signs include unusual thirst, frequent urination (especially at night), unexplained weight loss or fatigue, blurred vision, and slow-healing cuts or recurrent infections.

Many people have no symptoms at all in the early stages, which is why routine screening matters — especially with a family history of diabetes or if you're overweight. A simple blood test can confirm the diagnosis early, before complications set in.
