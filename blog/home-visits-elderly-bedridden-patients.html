Home Visits for Elderly & Bedridden Patients

Yes — home visits are available by appointment for patients who are unable to travel to clinic, including elderly or bedridden patients in Centre de Flacq, Rivière du Rempart, and surrounding areas. This allows for a proper physical assessment and prescription review in the comfort and safety of home.

This is arranged directly by phone or WhatsApp so the visit can be scheduled around the patient's needs.

Request a home visit on WhatsApp →

Note: in case of acute chest pain, sudden paralysis, or severe difficulty breathing, always call SAMU (114) immediately rather than waiting for a scheduled visit.
