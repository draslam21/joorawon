About

A holistic approach, built on two continents of training.

Dr. Aslam Joorawon is an Internal Medicine Specialist and Consultant with over 20 years of clinical experience, dedicated to comprehensive, patient-centred care. He has a particular interest in infectious diseases and rheumatological conditions, developed over years of hospital and private practice.

He graduated from Manipal University in India, and later furthered his training at Université de Bordeaux in France — combining a strong international foundation with extensive hands-on clinical experience in Mauritius.

As an internal medicine specialist, he takes a whole-patient approach to a wide range of conditions, offering careful assessment and treatment tailored to each individual, rather than the complaint alone.
